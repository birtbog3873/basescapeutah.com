# Google Ads Negative Keyword Master List — BaseScape / Home Services Contractors

> Generated 2026-04-02 | Wasatch Front, Utah market | Budget context: ~$400/mo, $5-12 CPC
> Import into Google Ads as a **Shared Negative Keyword List** and apply to all campaigns

## Match Type Strategy

**Default: Phrase Match** for most negative keywords. Phrase match blocks the search whenever the phrase appears in any order within the query, including with additional words. This is the right balance between coverage and not over-blocking.

**Use Exact Match** only when the word has dual meaning and you only want to block the specific standalone query (e.g., [amazon] blocks "amazon" but not "amazon delivery to my house").

**Use Broad Match Negative** sparingly — it blocks any query containing ALL the words in any order, which can accidentally exclude good traffic.

> In this document:
> - Keywords listed plain = **add as phrase match** (recommended default)
> - Keywords marked with `[brackets]` = **add as exact match only** (context-dependent)
> - Keywords marked with `{braces}` = **broad match negative** (use with caution)

## Implementation Checklist

1. **Create shared negative keyword lists** in Google Ads (Settings > Shared Library > Negative Keyword Lists)
2. **Name lists by category** (e.g., "NEG - Jobs/Careers", "NEG - DIY", "NEG - Geographic")
3. **Apply all lists to every campaign** — including Search, Performance Max, and Display
4. **Review Search Terms Report weekly** for the first month, then bi-weekly
5. **Start with phrase match** for everything unless noted otherwise above
6. **Never negate high-intent modifiers** like "cost", "price", "estimate", "near me", "quote" as phrase match
7. **Monitor for over-blocking** — check impression share and ensure you're not accidentally excluding good queries
8. **Budget context:** At $400/mo and $5-12 CPC, you get 33-80 clicks/month. Every wasted click = 1-3% of monthly budget gone

## Expected Impact

Based on industry data for home services contractors:
- **20-30% reduction** in wasted spend within the first month
- **45%+ improvement** in cost per lead after full implementation
- **67% lower CPA** reported by accounts using 200+ strategic negatives vs. minimal negative management
