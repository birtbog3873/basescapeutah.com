Scope of Work

BaseScape will design and construct a code-compliant walkout basement entry on the south-facing elevation of the residence, including all excavation, structural framing, waterproofing, concrete flatwork, and finish grading. The completed walkout will provide a separate exterior entrance suitable for an accessory dwelling unit (ADU) or rental suite.

  • Site excavation and soil retention (approx. 12 linear feet)
  • Structural engineering and load-bearing header installation
  • Foundation wall cut, reinforcement, and waterproof membrane
  • Exterior door rough-in and installation (36" insulated entry door)
  • Concrete landing, steps, and approach walkway
  • Drainage system with French drain and sump tie-in
  • Finish grading and site restoration
  • All required permits and inspections

Why This Is an Investment, Not an Expense
A code-compliant walkout entry enables a legal basement apartment generating $1,200 – $2,000/month in rental income — a 1.5 – 2.5% gross monthly ROI on this project. Most homeowners recoup the full investment within 18–24 months. Beyond cash flow, a finished walkout adds an estimated $40,000–$60,000 in appraised property value.
