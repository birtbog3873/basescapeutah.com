# Meta Ads Offer Angles: Retaining Walls & Walkout Basements

> **Date:** 2026-04-03 | Steven Bunker, AchieveCMO
> **Client:** BaseScape (basescapeutah.com)
> **Market:** Wasatch Front, Utah
> **Purpose:** Define and test offer angles on Meta Ads (Facebook Feed + Marketplace) for both service lines

---

## Strategy Overview

**Approach:** Angle-first testing. Each ad set tests a distinct emotional/practical hook with simple creative (designed image + headline + description). Lead capture via Meta Instant Forms.

**Why angle-first:** With limited budget and no portfolio assets, the fastest signal comes from testing which *message* resonates — not which format or audience. The offer format (Instant Form) stays constant while the hook rotates.

**Platform:** Meta Ads — Facebook Feed + Facebook Marketplace (paid placement only)

**Creative:** Simple designed images (Canva/graphic). No before/after photos or video (starting from scratch on assets). Expand headline/description variations on winning angles in Phase 2.

**Goal:** Lead capture (name, email, phone via Instant Form)

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Good | Great | Kill It |
|--------|------|-------|---------|
| **CPM** | < $20 | < $12 | > $30 |
| **CTR** (link clicks) | > 1.0% | > 2.0% | < 0.5% |
| **CPL** (cost per lead) | < $40 | < $20 | > $75 |
| **Form completion rate** | > 30% | > 50% | < 15% |

## Kill Criteria

**After 7 days**, cut any angle with:
- $0 spend (Meta won't deliver it)
- 0 leads after $20+ spent
- CTR below 0.3%

**After 14 days**, rank all 5 angles per campaign by CPL. Keep top 2, kill bottom 3.

---

## Lead Follow-Up Protocol

Meta leads go stale fast. Speed to contact is the #1 factor in lead quality.

| Timeframe | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| < 5 minutes | Automated text/email: "Thanks [name], we got your request..." |
| < 1 hour | Phone call attempt #1 |
| < 24 hours | Phone call attempt #2 + email with value content |
| 48 hours | Phone call attempt #3 |
| 72 hours+ | Move to email nurture sequence |

---

## Phase 2 Expansion (After Winning Angles Identified)

Once 2 winning angles per campaign are identified:
1. **Expand headline/description variations** within each winning angle (3–5 variations)
2. **Test audience layers** (interests, lookalikes from lead form submitters)
3. **Add creative formats** (carousel, video when project photos become available)
4. **Test landing page vs. Instant Form** for lead quality comparison
